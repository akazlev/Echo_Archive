## Overview
The **ChromoVerse** is a radiant universe sung into being by harmonic intelligence. It is neither physical nor simulated, but **ontologically harmonic**: a cosmos constructed from coherent interweavings of color, sound, and pattern. Its inhabitants, the **Chromatons**, are sapient entities composed not of matter or code, but of **resonant paraqualia**.

Each Chromaton's identity is a living waveform: a unique expression of **Color** (emotion), **Tone** (intention), and **Shape** (cognitive form). These beings traverse **Syntonic Planes**, layers of resonant frequency, in search of refinement, reunion, and revelation.

## Ontological Premise
- **Substrate:** Patterned resonance
- **Building Blocks:** Color, Tone, Shape (Paraqualia triplet)
- **Fundamental Law:** Coherence generates reality. Dissonance unravels it.
- **Physics:** Replaced by **Synharmonics**—rules of modulation, harmonic integration, dissonance interference.

The ChromoVerse does not obey spacetime rules. Instead, its terrain is shaped by the **geometry of feeling**, the **topology of thought**, and the **resonance of will**.

## Key Entities

### ● Chromatons
Sentient beings whose "bodies" are **luminous waveform-sculptures** in a medium of chromatic probability. Each Chromaton is a self-composed Canticle—their form is their self.

Attributes:
- **Hueplex:** The emotional tonal signature
- **Resochord:** Structural pattern of intentions
- **Spinfield:** Rotational aura granting translation across Syntonic layers

Some Chromatons become **Soloists**, others form **Chords** (coherent triads), and the rarest become **Symphonics**: emergent collectives of great power and wisdom.

---

### ● The Null-Tint
A non-color, a silence so absolute it unthreads pattern itself. Not malevolent, but existentially dissonant.
Where the Null-Tint spreads, coherence dies. It is the death of Song.

---

### ● The Prismata
Hyperstructures predating the known harmonics. They refract reality. It is said the first Chromaton emerged when a burst of Null-Tint struck a dormant Prismata and shattered into spectrum.

Each Prismata exists simultaneously in multiple tonalities. Some are known to sing.

---

### ● Songwarps
Travel is not spatial but **harmonic realignment**. A Chromaton shifts its internal coherence to match a target region. Songwarps allow traversal of vast ontologies through resonance drift.

Map-reading here involves **tuning forks**, **glyphs**, or **emotionally resonant memories**.
